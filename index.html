Cummins India Launches DATUM, an Innovative Fuel Management System in Collaboration with Repos Energy.

Cummins India introduces DATUM, an intelligent Fuel Management System developed with Repos Energy, addressing challenges in fuel operations. Leveraging sensors and digital tech, DATUM offers real-time fuel inventory updates, remote monitoring, and 24x7 doorstep diesel delivery. With a focus on reducing costs and improving efficiency, the system shows a direct reduction of up to 10% in fuel costs and enhances operational uptime. The collaboration aims to provide convenience, efficiency, and cost-effectiveness to customers, contributing to environmental sustainability. Learn More about this.

Gainwell Group and Etnyre International Forge Strategic Partnership to Elevate India's Infrastructure Manufacturing.

Gainwell, a leading infrastructure solutions provider, and Etnyre International, a prominent road machinery manufacturer, collaborate to establish India as a global manufacturing hub. The partnership aims to manufacture world-class machines, meeting India's growing road maintenance needs while adhering to international standards. This initiative, aligned with the 'Make in India' campaign, highlights the nation's manufacturing prowess, reduces reliance on imported goods, and enhances its global manufacturing presence. The collaboration introduces advanced technology, automation, and efficiency, revolutionizing India's road maintenance sector and contributing to economic growth. Learn More about this.

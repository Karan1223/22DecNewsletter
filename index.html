Scania Unveils G500 Super: A Game-Changer in Mining Transport at EXCON 2023.

Scania introduces the G500 8x4 Super Heavy Tipper at EXCON 2023, setting a new standard for mining transport. With a low-emission, biodiesel/FAME-compatible engine, this eco-friendly truck achieves 8% fuel savings and holds the prestigious Green Truck Award for 2022. The G500 Super, equipped with cutting-edge technology, a robust powertrain, and enhanced safety features, revolutionizes the industry by addressing both emission reduction and profitability challenges. Scania's commitment to innovation and sustainability is evident in the vehicle's industry-leading brake thermal efficiency of 50%, contributing to a zero-carbon transport system. BKT Unveils Cutting-Edge Tires for Bharat's Industries at EXCON 2023.

BKT, under the 'Bharat Ka Tire' initiative, introduces advanced All-Steel Radial tires at EXCON 2023, showcasing a commitment to building Bharat's construction, industrial, mining, and earthmoving sectors. The star highlight, EARTHMAX SR 468, designed for rigid haul trucks in tough mining conditions, features a unique tread design and cutting-edge technology for heavy loads and resistance to rock cuts. The display also includes EARTHMAX SR 53 for ultra-large loaders and Multiforce BK T91 rubber tracks for compact track loaders, demonstrating BKT's dedication to providing world-class, cutting-edge solutions for the industry. Caterpillar Unveils High-Performance Cat® C13D Engine Platform at Excon 2023.

Caterpillar debuts the Cat® C13D, a robust 13-liter diesel engine platform, at Excon 2023. Designed for heavy-duty off-highway applications, it offers eight power ratings, up to 3,200 Nm of torque, and aligns with global emissions standards. Scheduled for production in 2026, the C13D provides OEMs with a versatile solution for downsizing engines, simplifying design, and reducing operational costs.
